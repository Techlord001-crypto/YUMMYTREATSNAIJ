Our Story

Yummytreatsnaija is more than just food – it's an experience of authentic Nigerian street flavors brought to your doorstep. From the smoky aroma of suya to the rich taste of Jollof rice, we deliver only the best, prepared with love and quality ingredients.

We started with one mission: to make Naija street food accessible, premium, and convenient for everyone. Whether you're craving pepper soup on a rainy day or small chops for a party, we’ve got you covered.
